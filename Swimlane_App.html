Time, Untangled.

Welcome to Swimlane — your continuous, infinite-canvas journal.

  • The Infinite Canvas: Traditional journals trap you in pages. Here, time is fluid. Scroll up into the past, or down into the future.
  • Projects & Notes: Click the 📁 or 📝 icons in a lane header to manage backlog tasks and long-form notes outside of the timeline. Tabs are fully draggable.
  • Task Context: Click the ▼ on any task to open an inline rich-text notebook directly on the timeline.
  • Contextual Flow: Opening a project isolates that lane and opens it alongside your calendar. Drag and drop project tasks directly onto calendar days to schedule them.
  • Command Palette: Press Ctrl + K (or Cmd + K) to instantly search your entire timeline.
Four item types — click the coloured badge on any item to cycle through:
T Task — everyday to-do. Right-click to mark done.
M Milestone — a key date you're working toward. Appears in the Horizon with a countdown.
O Outcome — a big goal with a target date. Displayed prominently in your lane.
E Event — something happening on a specific date (meeting, release, trip).
